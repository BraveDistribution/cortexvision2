Our Products

Discover our cutting-edge products designed to meet your needs. We offer a range of solutions built with the latest technology.

  • ABIGAIL

    ABIGAIL is a medical software solution that enables noninvasive MR classification of brain tumors, eliminating the need for biopsy. It facilitates direct initiation of radiotherapy or chemotherapy in cases where gross surgical resection is not feasible.

  • BRAIN

    BRAIN is a medical software solution designed to prioritize the evaluation of positive traumatic head CT scans by on-call radiologists, reducing the time from imaging to treatment initiation.

  • BRIDGE

    BRIDGE is a software infrastructure designed to enhance medical data governance and exploration. Its goal is to improve the quality and efficiency of healthcare delivery while establishing a solid foundation for high-quality medical research The first milestone with the BRIDGE project is CLEAN – Clinical Language Engine for Automated Narratives, a solution aimed at automating discharge summary generation and assisting with DRG coding through large language models, thereby reducing physicians’ administrative burden, improving documentation quality, and enhancing hospital reimbursement accuracy.

Peter Drotar

Peter Drotar is a distinguished Professor at the Technical University of Kosice, specializing in machine learning, medical imaging, and biomedical signal processing. An ESET Science Award finalist and former Honeywell scientist, he leads impactful research and development projects applying artificial intelligence to advance diagnostic technologies and intelligent information systems.

Jakub Gazda

Jakub Gazda, MD, is an internist at the 1st Department of Internal Medicine, Pavol Jozef Šafárik University, with extensive research focused on liver diseases, including their pathogenesis, treatment, and epidemiology. He actively contributes to advancing medical knowledge, including the exploration of artificial intelligence to enhance diagnostic capabilities in clinical practice.

Matej Gazda

Matej Gazda is an accomplished AI researcher and PhD candidate at the Technical University of Kosice, focusing on innovative applications of artificial intelligence in medical imaging, including advanced image segmentation and diagnostics.
